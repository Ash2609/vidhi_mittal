Liquor Policy re-written; Drinking age reduces from 25 to 21 in Delhi

New Delhi, Dec 06, 2021

The legal age to drink in Delhi will now be 21, Delhi Deputy Chief Minister Manish Sisodia announced calling the move pragmatic and progressive. He said there would be no government liquor stores in Delhi, and no new liquor shops will not be opened in the national capital. Earlier, the legal age to consume liquor in Delhi was 25.
